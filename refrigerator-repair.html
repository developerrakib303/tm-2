Common refrigerator faults we fix

Not cooling

Warm inside — compressor or gas fault.
Freezer too warm

Freezer soft or defrosting on its own.
Leaking water

Water pooling inside or under the fridge.
Loud or constant noise

Buzzing, humming or rattling compressor.
Ice & frost build-up

Frost in the freezer or blocked vents.
Won’t turn on

No power, tripping or a dead panel.
Door won’t seal

Worn gasket letting cold air escape.
Dispenser / ice maker

Water or ice dispenser not working.
