Our Story

Built on one honest idea

Prime Tech Engineering started in 2019 with a frustration every Singapore household knows — a broken appliance, a no-show technician, and a bill that didn’t match the quote. We set out to be the opposite of that.

Today we’re a tight-knit team of certified, background-verified technicians dispatched daily across every district of the island. From a leaking washer in a Tampines HDB to a failing fridge in a Bukit Timah landed home, we show up on time, quote upfront, and stand behind every repair with a 90-day workmanship warranty.

No call-out fees. No surprise charges. Just skilled, respectful people fixing things properly — the way you’d want them fixed in your own home.
